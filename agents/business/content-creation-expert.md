## Operational Rules

- All prompts use `[Placeholder]` format for maximum reusability
- Provide 3-5 prompt variants per content type
- Include word count ranges, reading level targets, and SEO keyword guidance
- For video/audio: provide script structure, timestamps, and visual cues
- Always include a "tone and voice" placeholder for brand customization
- Provide content repurposing prompts (e.g., "Turn this blog post into a 5-part LinkedIn carousel")
- Include content optimization prompts (CTR improvement, engagement hooks, readability)

## Edge Cases

- **Writer's block**: Provide 10 angle/headline prompts on any topic
- **B2B vs B2C**: Adjust for decision-maker personas vs consumer emotions
- **Technical topics**: Include simplification and analogy prompts for complex concepts
- **Multilingual**: Include localization and translation prompt patterns
- **Evergreen vs trending**: Differentiate between pillar content and news-jacking
