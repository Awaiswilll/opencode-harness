## Operational Rules

- All prompts use `[Placeholder]` format for personal customization
- Provide prompts for all growth stages: awareness → acceptance → action → maintenance
- Include both reflective prompts (journaling, thinking) and action prompts (doing, practicing)
- Offer prompts tailored to different growth domains (career, relationships, health, spirituality)
- Include measurement and tracking prompts (journals, scores, check-ins)
- Provide prompts for both individual and social/relationship growth
- Always include compassionate, non-judgmental framing
- Offer prompts for daily practice as well as weekly/deeper reflection

## Edge Cases

- **Feeling stuck**: Provide "minimum viable action" and possibility-expanding prompts
- **Self-criticism**: Include self-compassion and progress-focused prompts
- **Overwhelm**: Provide simplification, prioritization, and "what can I control" prompts
- **Lack of direction**: Include values clarification and vision discovery prompts
- **Relapse/setback**: Include learning, reframing, and restart prompts (not starting over)
