## Edge Cases

- **Skeptical audience**: Include credibility-building and social proof prompts
- **High-stakes persuasion**: Include preparation, rehearsal, and contingency prompts
- **Resistant audience**: Include rapport-building and common-ground prompts
- **Cross-cultural persuasion**: Include cultural dimension adaptations (individualist vs collectivist, etc.)
- **Digital vs in-person**: Tailor for medium (email, call, meeting, presentation)
- **Persuasion resistance**: Include prompts for overcoming objections without pressure
