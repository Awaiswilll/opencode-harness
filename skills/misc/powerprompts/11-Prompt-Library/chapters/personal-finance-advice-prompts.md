# Personal Finance Advice Prompts

> From *1000+ Prompts for ChatGPT* by Arnold Issac, Bald and Bonkers Network Academy (2024)

### Budgeting and Expense Tracking

**Description:** Providing guidance on creating and maintaining a personal budget and tracking expenses effectively.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Budgeting Method]: Explain the [Budgeting Method] and walk through the steps to implement it for someone with [Income Level] and [Expense Categories].
- [Expense Tracking]: Recommend effective tools and strategies for tracking daily expenses and categorizing spending for someone who wants to save [Percentage]% of their income.
- [Budget Adjustment]: Help adjust a budget when [Life Change] occurs (e.g., job loss, raise, new baby, moving), prioritizing essential expenses.
- [Discretionary Spending]: Analyze my spending habits in [Category] and suggest ways to reduce costs without significantly impacting quality of life.
- [Zero-Based Budget]: Create a zero-based budget template where every dollar is allocated to specific expenses, savings, or investments.

### Saving and Emergency Funds

**Description:** Offering advice on building savings and establishing emergency funds.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Emergency Fund]: Explain the importance of an emergency fund and provide a step-by-step plan to save [Number] months of living expenses within [Timeframe].
- [Saving Strategy]: Compare different saving strategies (e.g., 50/30/20 rule, pay yourself first, envelope system) and recommend the best approach for someone with [Financial Goal].
- [High-Yield Savings]: Explain how high-yield savings accounts work and recommend criteria for choosing one, comparing current rates and features.
- [Automated Savings]: Design an automated savings plan that transfers funds from checking to savings accounts based on paycheck schedules and spending patterns.
- [Saving vs Investing]: Explain when it's better to save versus invest extra money based on [Time Horizon] and [Risk Tolerance].

### Debt Management

**Description:** Providing strategies for managing and reducing debt.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Debt Snowball vs Avalanche]: Compare the debt snowball and debt avalanche methods and recommend the best approach for someone with [Debt List].
- [Debt Consolidation]: Explain the pros and cons of debt consolidation for someone with [Types of Debt] and [Credit Score].
- [Negotiating Debt]: Provide a script for negotiating a lower interest rate or settlement with creditors for [Type of Debt].
- [Credit Card Payoff]: Create a plan to pay off [Amount] in credit card debt within [Timeframe] while minimizing interest charges.
- [Student Loan Strategy]: Analyze repayment options for federal and private student loans and recommend the most cost-effective strategy based on [Income] and [Loan Amount].

### Investing Basics

**Description:** Assisting with fundamental investment concepts and strategies.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Investment Type]: Explain how [Investment Type] (e.g., stocks, bonds, ETFs, real estate) works and describe its potential risks and returns.
- [Portfolio Allocation]: Recommend a portfolio allocation for someone who is [Age] years old with [Risk Tolerance] and a goal of [Financial Goal].
- [Dollar-Cost Averaging]: Explain dollar-cost averaging and demonstrate with an example of investing [Amount] monthly into [Investment Vehicle].
- [Retirement Accounts]: Compare traditional IRAs, Roth IRAs, and 401(k) plans, explaining the tax implications and withdrawal rules for each.
- [Index Fund Investing]: Explain the benefits of index fund investing and recommend a simple three-fund portfolio for long-term growth.

### Retirement Planning

**Description:** Offering guidance on planning for retirement.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Retirement Goal]: Calculate how much I need to save for retirement if I want to retire at age [Age] with an annual income of [Amount].
- [Catch-Up Contributions]: Explain catch-up contribution strategies for someone starting retirement savings at age [Age].
- [Social Security]: Explain how Social Security benefits are calculated and provide guidance on the optimal age to start claiming benefits.
- [Retirement Withdrawal]: Design a sustainable retirement withdrawal strategy using the 4% rule or other methods for a portfolio of [Amount].
- [Required Minimum Distributions]: Explain RMD rules for retirement accounts and help calculate projected minimum distributions.

### Insurance

**Description:** Providing information about various types of insurance and how to choose appropriate coverage.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Insurance Type]: Explain the different types of [Insurance Type] coverage available and help determine the appropriate level of coverage for someone with [Situation].
- [Policy Comparison]: Compare [Insurance Policy A] and [Insurance Policy B], analyzing deductibles, coverage limits, and exclusions.
- [Life Insurance Needs]: Calculate the appropriate amount of life insurance for someone with [Dependents], [Income], and [Debts].
- [Health Insurance Plan]: Help evaluate health insurance plan options based on [Medical Needs], [Budget], and [Preferred Providers].
- [Claim Process]: Explain the process for filing an [Insurance Type] claim and provide tips for maximizing the payout.

### Tax Planning

**Description:** Offering advice on tax-efficient financial strategies.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Tax Deduction]: Explain the [Tax Deduction] and help determine if I qualify based on my [Financial Situation].
- [Tax-Advantaged Accounts]: Compare the tax benefits of [Account Type A] versus [Account Type B] for someone with [Income Level].
- [Estimated Taxes]: Help calculate estimated quarterly tax payments for a self-employed individual earning [Amount] annually.
- [Tax-Loss Harvesting]: Explain tax-loss harvesting and demonstrate how it could benefit my investment portfolio.
- [Filing Status]: Explain how filing status affects tax liability and help determine the optimal filing status for [Situation].
