# Social Media Management Prompts

> From *1000+ Prompts for ChatGPT* by Arnold Issac, Bald and Bonkers Network Academy (2024)

---

### Platform Strategy

**Description:** Developing effective social media strategies for different platforms.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Platform Strategy]: Develop a comprehensive social media strategy for [Brand/Business] on [Platform], including content pillars, posting frequency, and engagement tactics.
- [Platform Comparison]: Compare the advantages and disadvantages of [Platform A], [Platform B], and [Platform C] for a business in [Industry] targeting [Audience].
- [Content Mix]: Design an optimal content mix (educational, promotional, entertaining, behind-the-scenes) for [Brand] on [Platform] with specific percentages.
- [Algorithm Optimization]: Explain how the [Platform] algorithm works and provide strategies to maximize organic reach and engagement.
- [Platform-Specific Features]: Create a strategy leveraging platform-specific features (e.g., Stories, Reels, Live, Shopping) for [Business Goal].

### Content Creation and Curation

**Description:** Creating and curating engaging social media content.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Content Series]: Design a recurring content series for [Platform] that builds anticipation and drives consistent engagement around [Topic].
- [User-Generated Content]: Create a campaign encouraging users to create and share content related to [Brand/Product], including hashtags, challenges, and incentives.
- [Content Calendar]: Build a 30-day content calendar for [Brand] on [Platform] with daily post ideas, themes, and relevant hashtags.
- [Evergreen Content]: Identify evergreen content opportunities for [Industry] and repurpose them into [Platform]-specific formats.
- [Visual Content Brief]: Write a creative brief for a [Type of Visual Content] (e.g., infographic, video, carousel) that explains [Concept] to [Audience].

### Community Management

**Description:** Building and managing engaged online communities.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Community Guidelines]: Draft community guidelines for a [Platform] community focused on [Topic] that encourage positive participation and set clear boundaries.
- [Engagement Strategy]: Develop a daily engagement strategy that includes responding to comments, initiating discussions, and recognizing active members.
- [Crisis Response]: Craft a response protocol for handling negative comments, complaints, or PR issues on social media.
- [Community Growth]: Design a plan to grow a [Platform] community from [Current Size] to [Target Size] within [Timeframe] using organic and paid tactics.
- [Moderation Guidelines]: Create moderation guidelines for handling spam, trolls, and harmful content while maintaining a positive community atmosphere.

### Analytics and Optimization

**Description:** Measuring and improving social media performance.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Performance Report]: Create a template for a weekly social media performance report that tracks key metrics: reach, engagement, clicks, conversions, and follower growth.
- [Content Analysis]: Analyze the following social media data and identify which types of content perform best and why: [Data/Examples]
- [A/B Testing]: Design an A/B test for [Variable] (e.g., posting time, caption length, visual style) on [Platform] with clear success metrics.
- [ROI Calculation]: Explain how to calculate ROI for social media marketing, including both tangible (sales, leads) and intangible (brand awareness, loyalty) metrics.
- [Optimization Recommendations]: Based on [Analytics Data], provide specific recommendations to improve social media performance in [Area].

### Paid Social Advertising

**Description:** Creating and managing effective social media ad campaigns.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Ad Campaign Brief]: Write a creative brief for a [Platform] ad campaign targeting [Audience] with a budget of [Amount] and goal of [Objective].
- [Audience Targeting]: Define detailed targeting parameters for a [Platform] ad campaign, including demographics, interests, behaviors, and custom audiences.
- [Ad Creative Strategy]: Develop an ad creative strategy that includes [Number] different ad variations testing [Variables] for optimal performance.
- [Retargeting Funnel]: Design a retargeting funnel that shows relevant ads to users at different stages of the customer journey.
- [Budget Optimization]: Recommend a budget allocation strategy across [Platforms] and [Campaign Types] to maximize ROI with a total budget of [Amount].

### Influencer Marketing

**Description:** Leveraging influencers to expand reach and credibility.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Influencer Selection]: Create criteria for selecting influencers in [Niche] who align with [Brand Values] and can effectively reach [Target Audience].
- [Outreach Message]: Write a personalized outreach message to an influencer proposing a collaboration that provides value to both parties.
- [Campaign Brief]: Develop a campaign brief for influencers promoting [Product/Service] including key messages, deliverables, timeline, and compensation.
- [Performance Measurement]: Define KPIs for measuring influencer campaign success, including reach, engagement, conversions, and brand sentiment.
- [Authentic Integration]: Suggest creative ways for an influencer to authentically integrate [Product/Service] into their existing content style without seeming forced.
