### Grammar and Punctuation

**Description:** Offering guidance on proper grammar and punctuation usage, including common errors and best practices.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Grammar Rule]: Explain the proper usage of [Grammar Rule] in writing and provide examples of correct and incorrect usage.
- [Punctuation Mark]: Discuss the correct usage of [Punctuation Mark] in various contexts and share tips for avoiding common punctuation errors.
- [Common Grammar Error]: Identify [Common Grammar Error] and explain how to avoid it in writing, with examples.
- [Sentence Structure]: Analyze the sentence structure of the following passage and suggest improvements for clarity and grammatical correctness: [Passage]
- [Subject-Verb Agreement]: Explain the rules of subject-verb agreement and provide examples of common mistakes to watch out for.

### Spelling and Vocabulary

**Description:** Providing advice on improving spelling and expanding vocabulary.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Commonly Misspelled Word]: Explain the correct spelling of [Commonly Misspelled Word] and provide tips for remembering it.
- [Word Choice]: Suggest more precise or impactful alternatives for the word [Word] in the context of [Writing Context].
- [Vocabulary Building]: Share strategies for expanding your vocabulary in [Language/Domain] and recommend resources for continued learning.
- [Homophones]: Explain the difference between the homophones [Homophone Set] and provide examples of their proper usage.
- [Spelling Rule]: Explain the spelling rule for [Spelling Rule] and provide examples of words that follow it.

### Sentence Structure and Clarity

**Description:** Discussing techniques for improving sentence structure and enhancing clarity in writing.

**Prompt Templates:**
- [Sentence Type]: Explain the characteristics and proper usage of [Sentence Type] in writing and provide examples.
- [Active vs Passive Voice]: Discuss the differences between active and passive voice and provide guidance on when to use each.
- [Parallel Structure]: Explain the concept of parallel structure and provide examples of correct and incorrect usage.
- [Run-on Sentence]: Identify the run-on sentence in the following passage and suggest ways to correct it: [Passage]
- [Dangling Modifier]: Identify and correct the dangling modifier in the following sentence: [Sentence]
- [Conciseness]: Rewrite the following passage to improve conciseness without losing meaning: [Passage]

**Prompt Templates:**
