## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Browse** the chapter relevant to your task
2. **Select** a prompt template that fits your need
3. **Replace** the `[Placeholders]` with your specific details
4. **Copy & paste** into ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM
5. **Iterate** and refine based on the output

### Example

**Template:**
> Write a compelling [Platform] ad copy that speaks directly to the pain
> points and desires of [Target Audience], positioning [Product/Service Name]
> as the ultimate solution.

**Filled version:**
> Write a compelling LinkedIn ad copy that speaks directly to the pain
> points and desires of B2B SaaS founders, positioning Powerpromtpts
> as the ultimate solution.
